Yet with less than a month remaining in the regular season, Houston (54-14) has a 2 1/2-game lead on the Warriors (52-17) in the West and Toronto is tied with them in terms of league-wide records. In that vein, here’s a question that would have seemed unfathomable a few months ago: Are Portland and Utah the only teams capable of putting a scare into the West’s elite come playoff time? It’s one thing for the fans and media to overlook these efforts, but the Warriors and Rockets would be well-advised to pay attention to the Jazz and Blazers.
